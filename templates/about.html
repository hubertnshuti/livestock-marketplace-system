Our Mission

Smarter Farming, Stronger Communities

We connect farmers and buyers through technology, building trust and transparency in livestock farming.

Our Story

We initiated this platform to solve real problems faced by farmers — livestock theft, disease, and unfair markets.

Our journey began around ItungoHub at UR‑CST in Kigali, where we saw how hard it is for small‑scale farmers to get fair prices and reliable information. By combining local field knowledge with modern web tools, we are building a more transparent livestock marketplace for Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every farmer in Rwanda — from Nyamirambo to Musanze and Nyagatare — has a simple digital place to showcase livestock, and every buyer can purchase with complete confidence about origin and health.
